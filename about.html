Storylog is for sharing true stories.

Storylog was dreamt up in a fortuitous bout of hocus pocus in the summer of 09.

It made its way onto the internet November 7th of the same year.

Stories submitted to Storylog are licensed under cc-wiki with attribution required.

What was the inspiration for Storylog?

Lowbrow 1.0 (1998-06), Davesecretary, PostSecret.

Why is there no text formatting like italics and bold?

Italics and bold have their uses, buy telling stories isn't one of them. If you really need to emphasize something, there are *many* other _ways_ to do that!!

Why did a story I saved disappear from my favorites?

It was deleted by its author. Authors have ultimate control of what happens to their stories on Storylog.

Why do story titles look stripped down on their author's profile page?

On profile pages story titles are stripped down to everything but letters and numbers. So _I'm a (story)!_ turns into _Im A Story_. I know this is kind of weird, but it makes Storylog a tiny bit more efficient. This might be corrected in the future.

How do I find new stories?
